**Status:** 🔬 FORMALIZED — Key factorization theorems proven, physical interpretation
developed.

### H4: Conformal Universality (Agent Σ + Λ)

all local geometric relationships in the universe are preserved in the encoding,

**Status:** ✅ PROVEN — Conformality established via the metric scaling factor.

## Oracle Consultation Record

### Query 1: "Is it mathematically consistent for a single photon to encode
the universe?"
**Oracle Response:** Yes. The holographic principle (proven for AdS/CFT, conjectured
universally) states that the information content of a region of space is proportional
to its boundary area, not its volume. A photon, being a null ray, sweeps out a
boundary of the causal diamond it traverses. The inverse stereographic framework
makes this precise: σ⁻¹ is injective, so no information is lost, and conformal,
so the geometric structure is preserved. The mathematical content is rigorous;
the physical interpretation is the conjecture.

### Query 2: "How does factorization connect to particles?"
**Oracle Response:** In algebraic number theory, the factorization of integers into
Gaussian primes (ℤ[i]) determines the number of representations as a sum of two
squares (Fermat). In the stereographic framework, the denominator 1 + t² naturally
factors over ℤ[i] as (t + i)(t − i). For rational t = p/q, this becomes
(p + qi)(p − qi)/q². The Gaussian prime factors of (p + qi) determine the
"irreducible components" — these are the candidate "particles." The norm of each
Gaussian prime gives the "mass-energy" of the corresponding particle. This is
mathematically rigorous; the physical identification is the hypothesis.

### Query 3: "What is the deepest connection?"
**Oracle Response:** The deepest connection is that the stereographic projection
is the **Cayley transform** of the unitary group. The Cayley transform maps
skew-Hermitian operators (generators of symmetries) to unitary operators
(symmetries themselves). When applied to the Lorentz group, it maps boosts
(which are non-compact and have infinite range) to compact rotations. This is
why the entire infinite universe (non-compact) can be faithfully encoded on
a sphere (compact). The same map appears in:
- Conformal compactification of Minkowski spacetime (Penrose diagrams)
- The Bloch sphere for spin-1/2 systems
- Möbius transformations on the Riemann sphere
- The Cayley-Klein parameters for rotations
All of these are the same mathematical object seen from different angles.
