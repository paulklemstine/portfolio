The Cayley-Dickson Tower

ℝ → ℂ → ℍ → 𝕆 — the only four normed division algebras, and why the pattern stops.

Hurwitz Theorem

Normed division algebras exist in dimensions 1, 2, 4, 8 — and ONLY those.

This is one of the deepest results in algebra. The project proves it by showing norm multiplicativity at each level and demonstrating the composition identities fail at dimension 16 (sedenions have zero divisors).
