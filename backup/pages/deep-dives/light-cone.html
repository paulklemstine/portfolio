Lorentz Geometry & Light Cone Theory

Pythagorean triples are photon momenta. The Berggren matrices are discrete Lorentz transformations.

The Key Insight

a² + b² = c²  ⟺  Q(a,b,c) = a² + b² − c² = 0  ⟺  (a,b,c) is a null vector

The Pythagorean equation IS the light cone equation in 2+1 Minkowski spacetime. Every Pythagorean triple is literally a photon momentum vector.

Berggren Matrices as Lorentz Transforms

Each Berggren matrix B satisfies BTQB = Q where Q = diag(1,1,−1). This is the defining property of the Lorentz group O(2,1).
