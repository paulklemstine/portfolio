Stereographic Projection & Universal Decoder

One formula connects everything: t ↦ ((1−t²)/(1+t²), 2t/(1+t²)).

The Single Formula

t  ↦  ( (1 − t²)/(1 + t²),   2t/(1 + t²) )

This one formula — stereographic projection from the real line to the unit circle — is the Rosetta Stone of the entire project. It connects:

  • Pythagorean triples: Setting t = n/m produces all PPTs
  • Weierstrass substitution: t = tan(x/2) unifies trigonometric integrals
  • Cayley transform: Maps upper half-plane to unit disk in ℂ
  • Bloch sphere: Parameterizes quantum states via stereographic S²
  • Farey fractions: Ford circle tangency ↔ Farey neighbor condition
  • Möbius transformations: Composition preserves determinant

Key Properties

INJ Injective on ℚ

Distinct rationals map to distinct circle points — no information is lost.

LIP Lipschitz Bounded

Both components are Lipschitz with constant ≤ 2, ensuring stability.

D2 D₂ Symmetry

The decoder has D₂ symmetry group, reflecting the four quadrants of the plane.

CR Cross-Ratio Invariance

Cross-ratio is preserved under Möbius maps — the fundamental projective invariant.
